Guides & Instructions

Resources to help students and teachers get the most out of this assessment tool.

Student Guide: Reading

For Students

Learn how to read critically, identify main ideas, evaluate evidence, and make connections between texts.

Skills covered: Central message, text structure, evidence evaluation, inference

Student Guide: Writing

For Students

Master the "Think First" framework to plan your writing and communicate your ideas clearly.

Skills covered: Main message focus, logical structure, grouping ideas, conventions

Teacher Assessment Guide

For Teachers

Detailed rubrics, assessment criteria, and feedback templates for evaluating student work.

Includes: Scoring guides, feedback prompts, tracking recommendations
