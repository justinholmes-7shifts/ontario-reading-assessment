Welcome to the Ontario Reading & Writing Assessment Tool

This tool is designed for Grade 7-8 students and is aligned with the Ontario Language Curriculum.

Reading Assessment

Comprehension & Analysis

Test your reading comprehension skills with passages including fiction, non-fiction, and media texts.

Skills assessed: Main idea, inference, vocabulary, critical analysis

Writing Assessment

Composition & Expression

Practice your writing skills with prompts for narrative, persuasive, informational, and response writing.

Skills assessed: Ideas, organization, voice, conventions

Ontario Curriculum Alignment

All assessments are designed to align with the Ontario Language Curriculum expectations for Grades 7 and 8, including:

  • Reading for meaning and comprehension strategies
  • Understanding form and style
  • Critical literacy and media analysis
  • Developing and organizing ideas in writing
  • Applying conventions of standard English

Achievement Levels

Results are reported using Ontario Achievement Chart levels:

  • Level 4 (80-100%): Exceeds provincial standard
  • Level 3 (70-79%): Meets provincial standard
  • Level 2 (60-69%): Approaching provincial standard
  • Level 1 (50-59%): Below provincial standard
